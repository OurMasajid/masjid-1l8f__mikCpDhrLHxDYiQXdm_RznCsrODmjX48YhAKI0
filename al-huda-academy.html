Under the commission of Masjid Al-Huda/I.C.C.C., Al-Huda Academy has been jointly established by Br. Ahmed Abuatieh, the Masjid Director, and Dr. Hala Asamarai, the School Principal. Al-Huda Academy is a community of learners where the individual matters. The purpose of Al-Huda Academy is to teach Muslim children Quran, Arabic, and Islamic Studies. The focus of Al-Huda Academy is to teach Arabic and Islamic Studies in an interactive, Humanistic, and Cooperative Learning Style, as well as to teach students Quran memorization in a differentiated and individualized manner. The staff consists of very dedicated individuals who are committed to the cause of contributing to the betterment of our children through Islamic education.
