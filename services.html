Our Services

Prayers

The five daily prayers are held in congregation in the mosque. See the prayer timesheet for details of Iqama and prayer times. Jumuah (Friday) prayer offers 2 services:

English at 12:00 PM
Arabic at 1:00 PM.

Halaqas

There are a number of ongoing halaqas throughout the week. Please check our Activities page for details.

Dawah & Outreach

The Center welcomes groups or individuals who wish learn about Islam, or to visit the Center. We also offer meetings for anyone who wishes to speak to a scholar for a purpose of learning , research or wishing to accept Islam. For appointment or visit, please fill out this form.

Marriage

Marriage (Nikkah) is performed according to the Islamic Shariah rules. The Center has a license from the State of Minnesota. An appointment should be scheduled in advance. For an appointment please 612-782-3883 or fill out this form. (A valid legal marriage license from the State of Minnesota is needed for all marriages).

Family Counceling

Counseling services are available in our Center for personal, family, and interpersonal issues.We will insha'Allah work to provide solutions to social problems; problems such as divorce, marriage counseling, parent-child relationship, neighborhood, friendship, etc. For discussing any social issue or problem with a Counselor, please contact:

Arbitration

Our Center provides a court-like service for the Muslim community. The Imam will work as a judge to resolve the dispute between any two parties or group. Cases might be social, family matters, business, or any kind of conflict. Based on the complexity of the case, we might organize an arbitration committee from experts to help in solving the problem and taking the decision. All the arbitration decisions are final.

Ramadan

In the blessed month of Ramadan, all the Halaqas will stop and we will have the following activities instead:
(The details and schedules for these activities will be announced at the time of Ramadan, and are subject to change)
• Breaking the Fast: Every night to break the fast: milk, dates, and fruits will be provided inshaAllah.
• Taraweeh prayer: Taraweeh prayer is held every night in congregation in the Mosque.
• Lecture: There will be a lecture every night after the first 4 raka’as of the taraweeh prayer
• Tahajjud prayer: At the last 10 days of Ramadan, The center organizes Tahajjud prayer and night dinners (Suhoor).

Eid Prayers

The Eid prayer (for the 2 Eids) will be organized in one of the convenient places in Minnesota. Please contact us to verify the location.
